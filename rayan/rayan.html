Hotel "Rayan" is a modern comfortable hotel in the center of Osh. Within two minutes of walking distance from the hotel there are the Osh City Hall, the T. Satylganov Park, the Izyum Restaurant, the Nur cinema and many other important objects and attractions. Hotels in Osh, as a rule, are adapted either for tourists or for traveling tourists. Due to its ideal location and high standards of comfort and service, the Rayan Hotel is ready to welcome both tourists and business guests.
